Overview of Features

MaxSearch puts you in control of your data. We captured the incredible success stories of how organizations used our application to ensure success in government, non-profit, and in the marketplace.

Instant Searches

The search function is simple and accurate. We save busy professionals time by providing them with the exact information they are searching for instantly. MaxSearch is being utilized in multiple industries such as government, education, and business, attorneys and accountants, and many more.

Integration and Compatibility

MaxSearch can index and interpret various types of metadata. MaxSearch integrates with a variety of platforms; CRM, ERM, SQL and No SQL databases, Tableau, R, Spark, Machine Learning and Artificial intelligence APIs.

Secure

We understand the importance of cyber security. MaxSearch is equipped with military-grade cyber security, adhering to DOD standards.

Simplicity

MaxSearch has a user interface that is easy to use. Unlike other platforms that take certificates and extensive training, MaxSearch can be implemented within a large organization with minimal training.
